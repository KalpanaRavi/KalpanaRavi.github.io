OUR MISSION STATEMENT:

Mega Force Security Services has been established by Ex Armed Forces Officer and managed by a team of Ex-Servicemen & Qualified Civilian Staff. It is headed by a Army Veteran who has vast experience in the field of security services having been Head of Security division of Factory manufacturing Arms, Ammunitions & Defence Equipments. As Regional Head of Tamil Nadu & Pondicherry of a Large Security company & later as CEO with another Large company, both having PAN India presence, was providing Security Services to clients ranging from Hospitality (5 Star Hotels), Banking and Insurance sector, IT sector, Energy sector, Hospitals, Corporate Offices etc. Armed Security Guards were being provided to Banks, Financial institutions and to 5 Star Hotels & IT Majors, Ex NSG Commandos were being provided in addition where required.

At Mega Force Security Services we firmly believe that there are three essential factors that
determine the quality of service provided by any supplier in the contract security industry.
They are:
• Management Response
• Employee Screening and Selection
• Training and Supervision

Great attention is paid towards recruitmet of personnel for Guarding Services before they are put through required training. Their physical condition and antecedents are verified. In addition to lessons on basic guarding skills, they are taught the rudiment of First Aid, Fire fighting and crisis management. They also go through on "On the Job training". Mega Force has trained instructors duly Accredited by SSSDC (Security Sector Skill Development Council) to impart Basic Training as well as OJT & also carryout assessment of Trained Security Guards.

Supervion is equally important for the provision of quality service which is ensured by an experienced Operational team comprising of Managerial & Field Staff, committed to providing round the clock fool proof security to customers.

AIM & OBJECTIVES:

• Provid round the clock Efficient & Fool proof Security to clients.
• Provide training,equipment and attractive wages to the guards, supervisors to ensure Professionalism,   Integrity & Continuity.
• Be a Leading Center of Excellence in management of Security, Investigation and Allied Services.
